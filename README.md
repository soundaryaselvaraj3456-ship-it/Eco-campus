# 🌱 EcoCampus AI – Smart Campus Sustainability Assistant

## 📌 Overview

EcoCampus AI is an AI-powered sustainability monitoring platform designed for colleges and universities. The project helps institutions track and reduce resource wastage related to electricity, water, and paper consumption while promoting environmentally responsible practices.

By combining Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning concepts, sustainability analytics, and interactive dashboards, EcoCampus AI provides actionable insights that support greener and smarter campuses.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Educational institutions consume significant amounts of resources every day. Without proper monitoring systems:

* Electricity wastage often goes unnoticed
* Water consumption remains untracked
* Excessive paper usage increases operational costs
* Carbon emissions continue to rise
* Sustainability awareness remains low among stakeholders

EcoCampus AI addresses these challenges through intelligent monitoring and data-driven recommendations.
