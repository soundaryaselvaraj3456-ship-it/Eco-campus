01 — The Challenge

Campus Resource Wastage

Colleges and universities consume enormous amounts of electricity, water, and paper every day. Without intelligent monitoring, this waste silently inflates costs, raises emissions, and undermines sustainability goals.

⚡

Electricity Waste

Idle labs, 24/7 HVAC, and unmanaged servers drain power around the clock even when buildings are empty.

~35% of energy wasted
💧

Water Waste

Leaking pipes, unmonitored irrigation systems, and ageing fixtures waste thousands of litres per day.

~28% lost to leaks
📄

Paper Waste

Mass printing of handouts, exam sheets, and notices generates avoidable paper waste and deforestation pressure.

~60% of prints unused
🌫️

Carbon Emissions

Cumulative resource waste from campus operations contributes significantly to annual institutional carbon footprints.

~4.2T CO₂ / campus / yr

03 — Our Approach

The Proposed Solution

Four tightly integrated AI modules that turn raw sensor data into actionable sustainability intelligence.

01
📡

Real-Time Resource Monitoring

IoT sensors stream live electricity, water, and paper data to a central dashboard. Anomalies surface instantly, giving facilities teams seconds — not hours — to respond.

02
📈

Predictive Consumption Trends

ML models trained on historical campus data forecast next-day and next-week usage, enabling proactive resource planning and budget optimisation.

03
🌿

Carbon Footprint Calculator

Converts raw consumption metrics into CO₂-equivalent scores per building, department, and campus — with trend comparisons against sector benchmarks.

04
🤖

Eco-Friendly AI Suggestions

IBM Granite–powered RAG chatbot delivers contextual, evidence-backed recommendations drawn from sustainability policies, campus data, and global best practices.
